About Me

I am a member of the class of 2024 at Washington and Lee University, majoring in European History and minoring in Digital Culture and Information and Law, Justice, and Society. When I graduate from Washington and Lee, I plan to go to law school so I can work as a lawyer pursuing justice system reform in the U.S. Department of Justice. At Washington and Lee, I have been involved in various extracurriculars, including playing on the women's varsity tennis team, being a co-facilitator for FYE-100, and being in the LEAD program. When I'm not in class or participating in my extracurricular activities, I enjoy watching sports, playing video games, and reading.
